About Us

Our Story

Elshadai Productions was founded with a vision to create powerful Christian media that inspires and transforms lives. Our journey began in the heart of Thoothukudi, Tamil Nadu, with a passion for worship and a commitment to excellence.

Today, we've grown into a full-service media production house, serving churches and Christian artists across India and beyond. Our team of dedicated professionals brings together technical expertise and spiritual sensitivity to create content that glorifies God and touches hearts.
